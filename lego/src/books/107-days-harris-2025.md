This one was painful to relive: the return to sanity that wouldn't be. My last social media post was a picture of my eldest, now 9, watching Kamala give an address as the newly sworn-in VP with the caption: Today I'm hopeful.

To say I'm still hopeful, today, would be closer to the subjunctive mood.
