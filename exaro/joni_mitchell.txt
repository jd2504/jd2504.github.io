Joni Mitchell, 2024-02-14

The urge to look away is instinctive, like you happened, by accident, on something private, deeply intimate, shared in confidence. But you can't help but to feel a part of that intimacy, wrapped in an embrace that isn't extended to you, but might be. It's out there in the world and it's yours. Or at least you feel at liberty to act as if it were.
